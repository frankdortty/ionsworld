About Us

Our History

IONS WORLD ENTERPRISE was a child of necessity in 2001. With the telecom revolution sweeping across the country there was a need for a one stop Information Communication Technology (ICT) shop, to meet the needs of members of the society. Since then IONS has expanded it's operations to food processing with products like IONS plantain flour, IONS peanut. IONS is a family oriented bussiness, staff,customers and vendors are considered and engaged as members of this family. The ICT arm of IONS world provides solution in hardware and software sales, Networking design and implementation, website design and programing. Traning in various pckages like Tally, Microsoft Dynamics, Programing languages.
